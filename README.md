Excited to share that I am currently working on the design and development of an Alumni Website for Peter Harvard INT'L School alongside my collaborator, Anointed Agunloye(Senior DevOps Enginner).

This platform is being built to create a centralized digital space where graduates can reconnect with their alma mater, stay informed about school activities, and celebrate the achievements of past students through a structured alumni community.

The project focuses on building a clean, modern, and scalable alumni management system that will be managed directly by the school administration, making communication and alumni engagement easier and more organized.

Grateful for the opportunity to collaborate on a project that strengthens long term connections between a school and its graduates.

More updates coming soon as development progresses.
